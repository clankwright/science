# Consumer health AI grounded in your own medical records
Vendors: Microsoft Copilot Health, Perplexity Health, OpenAI ChatGPT Health, Anthropic Claude for Healthcare, Amazon Health AI | Date: 2026-01..2026-03 | Maturity: production/beta
URL: https://iapp.org/news/a/the-health-ai-agent-rush-five-products-in-three-months-and-the-privacy-questions-that-got-left-behind

- Links a user's actual patient-portal records, labs, medications, and wearables, then answers questions grounded in their own history: plain-language lab summaries, visit prep, medication/allergy-aware guidance.
- ChatGPT Health (Jan 7) anchors the wave; Copilot Health (Mar 12), Perplexity Health (Mar 19) follow.
- US; connect EHR via third-party intermediaries (b.well, HealthEx, state HIEs) plus Apple Health/Fitbit. Free-to-low; several bundled with existing subs.
- New consumer tier distinct from clinician-facing OpenEvidence and lab-testing Function Health: AI reasoning over your own EHR data. Privacy questions flagged (records leave HIPAA-covered context).
