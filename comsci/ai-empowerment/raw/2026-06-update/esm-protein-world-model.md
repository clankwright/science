# ESM Protein Biology World Model (ESMC + ESMFold2 + ESM Atlas)
Vendor: Arc/Biohub (EvolutionaryScale/ESM lineage) | Date: 2026-05 | Maturity: production/research
URL: https://biohub.org/news/world-model-of-protein-biology/

- Open platform combining ESMC (language model on 2.8B sequences), ESMFold2 (structure prediction for proteins and complexes), and ESM Atlas (6.8B-sequence search).
- Predicts 3D structure and designs functional protein binders shown to work in the lab.
- Free, open to the global scientific community; web access + searchable 6.8B-sequence atlas; no enterprise gate.
- Ships structure prediction AND therapeutic-affinity binder design in one free model, where covered tools split the job (AlphaFold structure, RFdiffusion design).
