# Pi (Inflection), BetterUp, Rocky.AI, and clinician-augmenting tools

**Captured:** 2026-05-03

## Pi (Inflection AI)

**Sources:**
- https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inflection_AI
- https://spectrum.ieee.org/inflection-ai-pi
- https://mykin.ai/resources/pi-ai-eaten-by-microsoft
- https://www.sectionai.com/blog/what-happened-to-inflection-and-pi
- https://techcrunch.com/2024/08/26/five-months-after-microsoft-hired-its-founders-inflection-adds-usage-caps-to-pi/
- https://www.cmswire.com/digital-experience/pi-the-new-chatbot-from-inflection-ai-brings-empathy-and-emotion-to-conversations/

Pi launched 2023 as the first major LLM tuned explicitly for empathic, supportive conversation rather than information retrieval. Mustafa Suleyman (DeepMind co-founder) led the product.

In March 2024 Microsoft hired Suleyman, Karen Simonyan, and most of the Inflection team in a $650M licensing deal. The remaining Inflection team pivoted to enterprise model licensing. The free Pi consumer app continued in a degraded form: usage caps added August 2024, no model upgrades, no new features. Effectively abandoned.

Pi is the cautionary product in the category: built well, well-funded, validated empathic-LLM as a product surface, and still failed to find a viable consumer business outside of an acquihire. The conversational pattern Pi pioneered now lives in ChatGPT's Voice mode and Claude's tone defaults.

## BetterUp AI Coaching

**Sources:**
- https://www.betterup.com/products/betterup-ai-coaching
- https://www.betterup.com/press/betterup-launches-ai-coaching
- https://www.betterup.com/powered-by-ai
- https://support.betterup.com/hc/en-us/articles/36688702739611-BetterUp-AI-Coach

BetterUp launched its AI coach in January 2025. Trained on 5M+ sessions from BetterUp's network of human ICF-credentialed coaches. Sold B2B; employees access via employer subscription.

Distinguishing claim: the AI is a layer on top of human coaching, not a replacement. BetterUp Grow blends AI-suggested practice between human sessions, progress tracking, and behavior-change prompts.

Limit: not directly purchasable by individuals. Enterprise pricing only. Useful for a wiki section on "what your employer might offer" but not "what you can buy yourself."

Direct-to-consumer AI coaching chatbot, freemium. Goal-setting, daily check-ins, leadership development prompts. Six selectable AI voices for spoken coaching sessions. Smaller user base than Pi-era consumer chatbots; monetization unclear.

## Clinician-augmenting (Eleos, Lyssn)

**Sources:**
- https://eleos.health/individual-therapy/
- https://eleos.health/blog-posts/eleos-2025-ai-innovation-compassionate-care/
- https://eleos.health/blog-posts/jmir-study-outcomes/
- https://www.lyssn.io/resources/insights/lyssns-2025-impact-in-behavioral-health-and-human-services/
- https://www.talkspace.com/blog/ai-tools-for-therapists/
- https://bhbusiness.com/2025/04/29/ai-could-be-the-unlikely-coach-training-the-next-generation-of-therapists/

These are NOT individual-facing apps; they are therapist tools. Worth noting in the wiki because they indirectly empower individual patients via better-resourced clinicians.

- **Eleos**: ambient session capture, generates payer-ready progress notes from audio or short text summaries. Used by ~40K providers covering ~1M patients in 2025. Reported 80% reduction in clinician documentation time (14 vs. 69 hours to file a note).
- **Lyssn**: session transcription and clinician-feedback platform; trains young therapists by analyzing fidelity to evidence-based modalities.

For the wiki: these belong in a "see also" footnote, not the main individual-empowerment shortlist. They expand human-therapist throughput; the individual still needs access to a therapist in the first place.

## Honest framing

The cleanest empowerment story in this group is Wysa/Woebot for self-administered CBT (own page). The empathic-companion category (Pi, ChatGPT Voice in tutoring/coaching mode) is helpful for some, dangerous for others, and the field still lacks clean consumer products that are both clinically validated and commercially viable.
