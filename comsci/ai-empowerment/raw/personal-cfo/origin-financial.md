# Origin Financial: SEC-registered AI advisor at consumer pricing

## What it is

Origin is a consumer-priced personal finance app ($99/yr, $1 promo first year at launch) that in September 2025 unveiled what it claims is the first SEC-regulated AI financial advisor. The regulatory status is the moat: it can give actual advice within the regulated framework, not just "education."

## Why "SEC-regulated" matters

In the US, only SEC- or state-registered investment advisors can give personalized investment advice. Anyone else (ChatGPT, a finance YouTube channel, your friend) is restricted to "educational content." The legal line matters because:

- A registered advisor has a fiduciary duty to act in your best interest.
- Liability and recourse exist if advice is bad.
- Compliance and supervision frameworks apply (audit trails, disclosure requirements, conflicts disclosure).

Most "AI financial advisor" products as of mid-2026 sidestep this by framing themselves as education. Origin is the first to register and operate as a regulated advisor with the AI as the advisor surface.

## Pre-AI baseline

- Online financial planning tool: free trial then $10-15/mo, no advice.
- One-shot CFP plan: $2,000-$5,000, no ongoing access.
- Robo-advisor: 0.25% AUM, allocation only, no planning conversation.
- Free LLM (ChatGPT, Claude): general advice, no data access, hallucinates tax law, no fiduciary status.

## Maturity

Production. SEC-registered as of Sept 2025. Scope is real. The "outperforms CFPs on the exam" claim is from internal benchmarking and is marketing-grade evidence; the regulatory registration is hard fact.

## Honest limits

- "Outperforms CFPs on the exam" is not the same as "outperforms CFPs in practice." Standardized exam questions are the easy case; the hard case is messy households with edge cases the user doesn't know to mention.
- The regulatory framework is new for AI advisors. The supervision and liability practice will be tested by some bad-advice incident, and the response will set the pattern.
- $99/yr forces a specific economic model: high volume, low touch. Don't expect Origin's AI to do hour-long planning conversations the way a $5k Range relationship does.
