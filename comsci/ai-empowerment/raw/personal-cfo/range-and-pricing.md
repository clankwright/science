# Range and the AI-augmented CFP tier

## What it is

Range is a flat-fee wealth management firm targeting high-income households ($200k+ income, typically with equity comp, multiple accounts, complex tax). It is not a pure-AI product; it is a hybrid where each member gets human CFP/CPA access plus an AI advisor named "Rai" that handles between-meeting questions on demand. Series C of $60M closed Nov 2025.

## Specific unlocks

- "Ask Rai whether to exercise your ISOs in November or wait for the new tax year, get a numerical answer against your actual cap-table and projected AMT in two minutes instead of waiting two weeks for an advisor meeting."
- "Get K-1, multi-state filing, and international tax handled inside the same membership that runs your retirement plan, no separate CPA contract."
- "Replace a 1% AUM advisor on a $1M portfolio ($10k/yr) with a $5,950 flat fee ($5k savings/yr at break-even, scales to $50k/yr at $5M AUA)."
- "Equity compensation modeling for tech / healthcare / startup execs: 10b5-1 plan modeling, NQDC, RSU vesting waterfalls, all in one dashboard."

## Pricing tiers (2026)

- Premium: $2,950/yr. Investment management (0% AUM), unlimited fiduciary advisor access.
- Platinum: $5,950/yr. Adds tax filing, direct indexing, strategic tax planning, alternatives allocation.
- Titanium: $9,950/yr. Most complex households; international tax, real estate, K-1s.

Range removed semi-annual billing in 2026; everything is annual. Existing customers grandfathered.

## Rai (the AI layer)

- Trained on 275 years of combined advisor experience from Range's CFP team.
- Scored 95% on CFP practice questions when benchmarked against frontier LLMs.
- Connects across cash flow, taxes, investments, equity compensation, retirement planning.
- Conversational interface. Available to all members regardless of tier.

The AI is not a replacement for the human team; it is a between-meeting answer engine. The pitch is: high-touch advisor service at a price point previously only available to family-office clients ($5M+ liquid).

## Pre-AI baseline

- Wirehouse advisor: 1-2% AUM. On $1M, that's $10k-$20k/yr; on $5M, $50k-$100k/yr.
- Boutique RIA: 0.5-1% AUM, often $250k account minimum.
- One-shot CFP plan: $2,500-$5,000, no ongoing relationship.
- Family office: 0.5-1% AUM, typically $10M+ minimum.

Range collapses the HNW advisor cost structure by going flat-fee. The AI lets a single human advisor cover more clients without losing responsiveness, which is how the unit economics work at $2,950/yr vs. percentage-of-AUM.

## Cost / access

$2,950-$9,950/yr. Income floor is implicit ($200k+); the value math doesn't work below that. AUA of $9.5B across 5,000+ HNW members across all 50 states; $400M directly under management (vs. held-away assets they advise on).

## Maturity

Production. Real fiduciary firm with $9.5B AUA. Rai is in production for all members. The hybrid model (AI + human CFP) is the firm's whole thesis, not an experimental layer.

## Where it sits in the stack

- Below: Origin ($99-156/yr), Boldin ($120/yr), ProjectionLab ($129/yr), DIY with Claude/ChatGPT.
- Above: Facet (~$2k-$8k/yr, similar tier), traditional fee-only CFPs ($2k-$5k retainer), wirehouses (1% AUM).

Range vs Facet: both flat-fee, both human + tech. Range emphasizes AI more; Facet emphasizes human relationship more.
