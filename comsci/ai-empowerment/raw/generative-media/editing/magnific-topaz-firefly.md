These tools sit alongside generators, doing edit / enhance / extend rather than synthesize-from-scratch. Often more important than generators in actual workflows.

## Magnific AI

**Vendor:** Magnific (acquired by Freepik 2024, "Magnific (formerly Freepik)").

**Specialty:** "creative upscaling." Uses latent diffusion to ask "what could this look like at higher resolution" and hallucinates plausible detail (skin pores, hair strands, fabric weave, background texture). Primary use is making AI-generated images look richer and more photographic.

**Pricing:** from $39/mo (2500 tokens). Two months free annual.

**Distinctive:** opinionated tool, not a faithful upscaler. Will change identities if Creativity slider is high. Best for stylized / artistic upscales of AI-generated source material.

## Topaz Photo AI (and Gigapixel, DeNoise, Sharpen)

**Vendor:** Topaz Labs (Dallas).

**Specialty:** restoration upscaling. Models trained to recover what was actually there in a degraded source. Face Recovery preserves identity. Used by photographers on real-world photos, not generated images.

**Pricing:**
- Photo AI subscription: from $12/mo cloud, or $199/yr full bundle.
- Migrated from $99 perpetual license to subscription in 2024-2025 (controversial among long-time users).

**Distinctive:** identity-preserving for real photos. Topaz is the photographer's tool; Magnific is the AI artist's tool.

## Adobe Firefly (and Generative Fill, Generate Video)

**Vendor:** Adobe.

**Subscription plans launched Feb 12 2025:** standalone Firefly subs (in addition to bundling in Creative Cloud).
- Standard: $9.99/mo, 2000 credits/mo (~20 five-sec AI videos).
- Pro: $29.99/mo, 7000 credits/mo.
- Annual: $100 / $300.

**What Firefly covers:**
- Generative Fill (Photoshop): inpaint with text prompt. The most-used AI image-editing feature in the world by install base.
- Generative Expand: extend canvas with AI fill.
- Generate Image (text-to-image, Firefly Image 3 / 4 models).
- Generate Video (Firefly Video Model, 2025).
- Generate Audio.

**Distinctive:** the integration story. Adobe trained Firefly on its own Stock library and licensed content, claims indemnification for commercial use. Lives inside Photoshop, Premiere, Illustrator where pros already work. Quality of standalone Firefly models behind FLUX / Imagen / gpt-image-1; quality of in-Photoshop Generative Fill is still industry-default for surgical edits.

## Maturity

All production. Generative Fill in particular has become the "AI image editing" default for working professionals.

## Individual empowerment

A small e-commerce shop uses Generative Fill to remove backgrounds and extend product photos for free (within a Creative Cloud sub they already had). A photographer rescues underexposed wedding shots with Topaz instead of a $500 retoucher gig. An AI artist uses Magnific to make Midjourney outputs print-ready at 8K resolution. None of this required hiring a retoucher / designer.
