## Vendor / access

Runway ML (NYC startup, founded 2018, ~$3B valuation as of 2025). Web app, mobile app, API.

## Models / timeline

- **Gen-2** (Mar 2023): the model that made AI video possible but not pleasant.
- **Gen-3 Alpha** (Jun 2024): first Runway model competitive with the wave (Sora demo, Kling, Luma).
- **Gen-3 Alpha Turbo** (late 2024): faster, cheaper.
- **Gen-4** (Mar 2025): focus on character / location / object consistency across scenes. The "set your look once, get a coherent shot sequence" model. Strong on physics simulation.
- **Gen-4 Turbo**: speed-optimized, 5 credits/sec.
- **Gen-4.5** (late 2025): quality jump at same speed and price as Gen-4.

## Pricing

Subscription:
- Standard: $15/mo ($12 annual)
- Pro: $35/mo
- Unlimited: $95/mo

Credits-based for generation. Gen-4 Turbo at 5 credits/sec maps to roughly $0.50-1.00 per second of 1080p video for most users.

API: per-second pricing, comparable to Veo 3 Fast.

## Distinctive trait

The professional-pipeline tool. Runway is the only major video AI vendor with deep editing primitives baked in: keyframing, camera-path control, motion brush, video-to-video, green-screen alpha, and a full timeline editor. Used by film and TV productions (Everything Everywhere All at Once, The Brutalist, late-night TV graphics).

Gen-4's character consistency is the best in class for narrative shorts where the same person has to appear across multiple cuts.

## Limits

- No native audio (still needs a separate ElevenLabs / Veo 3 pipeline for sound).
- Max clip length 10 sec for most models.
- Quality below Veo 3 / Sora 2 on raw photorealism per benchmark; closer on controllability.

## Maturity

Production. Most film-industry-ready of the video AI vendors.

## Individual empowerment

An indie filmmaker storyboards an entire short film in a weekend, generating shots with consistent characters across 30+ scenes. A music video director who would have hired a $50k production crew now spends $200 in Runway credits. A solo animator produces motion-design B-roll that previously required After Effects expertise.
