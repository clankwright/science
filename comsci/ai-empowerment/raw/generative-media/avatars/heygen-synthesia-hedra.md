These tools take a script + an avatar (pre-built, photo-cloned, or video-cloned) and generate talking-head video. They sit between video generation and voice synthesis; usefulness is for explainer videos, training content, sales videos, AI influencers.

## HeyGen

**Vendor:** HeyGen Inc. (Los Angeles).

**Models / features:**
- 230+ pre-built avatars across age/ethnicity/wardrobe.
- Custom avatars from a photo or short video upload.
- 175+ languages with voice cloning included on Creator plan.
- **Avatar IV** (Aug 2025): full-body motion, micro-expressions, hand gestures matched to script tone. The current state of the art for talking-head realism.

**Pricing:**
- Free: 3 min/mo
- Creator: $29/mo ($24 annual) — unlimited videos, 1080p, no watermark, 30 min max length.
- Pro: $99/mo
- Enterprise: custom

Avatar IV restricted to 5 min/mo on Creator (the catch); add-on packs $15 / 300 sec.

**Distinctive:** highest realism in pre-built-avatar space; the current consumer leader in 2025.

## Synthesia

**Vendor:** Synthesia (London, founded 2017, the original enterprise avatar vendor).

**Pricing:**
- Free: 3 min/mo, 9 avatars
- Starter: $29/mo, 10 min
- Creator: $89/mo, 30 min, 180 avatars, 5 personal avatars
- Enterprise: custom, $1k-3k+/mo
- Custom Studio Express-1 avatar: $1000/year add-on

**Avatars:** 230+ stock, 140+ languages, 400+ voices.

**Distinctive:** the enterprise / corporate-training default. Used by 60%+ of Fortune 100 for internal training videos. Strong compliance, avatar-likeness controls, audit trails. Less cinematic than HeyGen Avatar IV but more polished for business use.

## Hedra (Character-3, Live Avatars)

**Vendor:** Hedra (San Francisco, founded 2023).

**Specialty:** the photo-to-talking-avatar workflow. Upload any image (a person, a cartoon, a painting), upload audio, get a talking video. Character-3 (current model) handles non-human characters and stylized art well, where HeyGen and Synthesia stick to realistic humans.

**Pricing:**
- Free: 100 credits/mo
- Basic: $15/mo
- Creator: $30/mo
- Professional: $75/mo
- All plans include commercial rights, no watermark.

**Live Avatars** (Jul 2025): real-time streaming talking-avatar at $0.05/min ($3/hr). Targets video-call avatars and interactive AI agents.

Single subscription gives access to 14 image and 14 video models, all from one credit pool.

**Distinctive:** the most flexible (any image as avatar source); the cheapest serious option; the only one with a real-time / live mode.

## D-ID

**Vendor:** D-ID (Israel, founded 2017).

The original photo-to-talking-head vendor (consumer side). Lost ground to HeyGen and Hedra on quality through 2024-2025. Still relevant for low-cost API integrations and "old photos brought to life" consumer products. Pricing from $5.99/mo Lite.

## Comparison

| Tool | Best for | Realism | Cost floor (paid) |
|---|---|---|---|
| HeyGen | Solo creator, marketing | Highest (Avatar IV) | $29/mo |
| Synthesia | Corporate training | High (polished) | $29/mo |
| Hedra | Stylized, real-time, flex | High (Char-3) | $15/mo + $0.05/min live |
| D-ID | Cheap API, legacy | Medium | $5.99/mo |

## Maturity

Production for talking-head explainer videos. Near-production for full-body motion (HeyGen Avatar IV pushing the frontier). Real-time avatars (Hedra Live) still preview / early production.

## Individual empowerment

A consultant produces a 50-language version of their sales pitch in an afternoon. A teacher records once, deploys in their students' native languages. A solopreneur creates a "spokesperson" for a product without ever appearing on camera. A creator launches a YouTube channel as an AI avatar (cost: $30/mo vs hiring an on-camera presenter at $500-2000/video).
