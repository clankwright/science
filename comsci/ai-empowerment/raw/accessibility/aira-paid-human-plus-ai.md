# Aira

## What it is

US-based commercial visual-interpreting service (founded 2014). Connects blind / low-vision users via live video to a *trained, paid, agreement-bound* sighted interpreter (called an "Aira Agent"), 24/7/365, no reservation. Distinct from Be My Eyes in that agents are professionals, not volunteers, and the relationship is a contract: agents follow scripts for sensitive tasks (medical, financial), are NDA-bound, and are explicitly trained on blindness etiquette.

## Specific unlocks

- **Free for 5 minutes every 48 hours** to non-subscribers: enough to read a piece of mail, navigate a kiosk, or get oriented in an unfamiliar building.
- **Free at "Access Partner" venues:** thousands of airports, museums, universities, transit systems, government buildings, hotels worldwide pay Aira to make calls free for blind visitors on their premises (geofenced). Examples in 2025-2026: Manitoba Museum, Syracuse University, Colorado state government employees, hundreds of US airports.
- **Hands-free on Ray-Ban Meta and Oakley Meta glasses** via Meta AI app; calls escalate from audio-only to WhatsApp video so the agent can see through the glasses' camera.
- **First call free + 60 free minutes** via the "Discover Aira Offer" first month.
- **Employment-seeking free minutes** for users navigating job search (resume review, interview prep, online application forms that defeat screen readers).
- **Build AI program:** opt-in data collection that gives users free minutes in exchange for letting Aira train AI models on their interactions.
- Tasks Be My AI cannot do: navigating a complex inaccessible web checkout, walking through an unfamiliar transit station, reading a small-print legal contract while a notary watches, identifying which of 10 visually-similar shampoo bottles the user wants in CVS.

## Pre-AI baseline

Aira pre-dates the LLM wave (founded 2014) and originally relied on Google Glass before pivoting to phones. The "AI augmentation" angle is recent: the agent now has AI tools at hand (text recognition, scene description) to accelerate certain tasks, but the core service is still a human in the loop. The unlock is not "AI replaces humans" but "AI extends free coverage" by handling the simple cases (Be My AI / Seeing AI) so Aira can focus on complex cases without burning user minutes.

## Cost / access

- **Pay-as-you-go and subscription tiers:** historical pricing started around $29/mo for ~30 minutes; current offering simplified to "minutes" subscriptions. Heavy users (employed full-time professionals) pay $200+/mo equivalents.
- **Free at Access Partner venues** (geolocated, app shows a banner when you enter a free zone).
- **5 free minutes / 48 hr** for any user, any location.
- US, Canada, UK, Australia, and a growing list of other markets. Agents are all US/Canada-based for the English service.
- iOS, Android, Windows desktop, Ray-Ban Meta, Oakley Meta, Envision Glasses.

## Maturity

**Production**, longest-running paid visual-interpreting service. The 2024-2026 evolution is integration with smart glasses (Meta's), expansion of Access Partner geofences, and Build AI data collection feeding model training. Aira's pivot is to position itself as the "human escalation tier" above AI: when GPT-4o cannot do it, ask Aira.

Limits: not free without partner geofence; agents only speak English (and Spanish in beta); not available in much of Europe / Asia.
