# Be My Eyes + Be My AI

## What it is

Be My Eyes is a Danish nonprofit (founded 2015) that pairs blind/low-vision users with sighted volunteers via video call. In March 2023 it launched **Be My AI** (initially "Virtual Volunteer"), the first production deployment of OpenAI's GPT-4 with Vision in an accessibility app: point your phone camera, get an immediate detailed description, ask follow-up questions, all with no human in the loop.

## Specific unlocks

- Read the contents of your fridge at 2am with no volunteer queue, no sighted human ever seeing inside your home.
- Identify a pill bottle and read the dosage instructions including the small print on the warning sticker, then ask "is this safe to take with grapefruit?"
- Get a full visual description of yourself before a video call: hair, clothing, lighting, background framing.
- Read handwritten notes (a sticky note, a child's drawing, a doctor's signature on a form).
- Describe a restaurant menu and answer "which of these is vegan and under $20?"
- On Ray-Ban Meta / Oakley Meta smart glasses, get hands-free scene description by voice command, with audio coming through open-ear speakers (announced Sept 2024, expanded March 2026).
- Connect hands-free via the smart glasses to a *trusted contact* (friend, family member) or to a brand-specific support team (e.g. specific bank's accessibility line) instead of a generic volunteer.
- Available on Windows desktop (2024 launch) for screen-content QA: describe a screenshot, an inaccessible web page, an image embedded in a PDF.

## Pre-AI baseline

Two options pre-2023: (a) wait for a Be My Eyes volunteer to pick up (median answer time was good but unpredictable; awkward for embarrassing or private content like medication, mail, body); (b) pay Aira ($29-129/mo subscription) for a trained agent. Neither was instant; both required exposing private visual content to a human.

For longer-form tasks (read an entire menu, summarize a multi-page document, identify all items in a fridge), volunteer calls were impractical: the human would lose patience or the call would time out. AI handles 20-minute back-and-forth conversations with no fatigue.

## Cost / access

**Free for blind/low-vision users.** Funded by OpenAI partnership and corporate access partners. Available on iOS, Android, Windows desktop, Ray-Ban Meta, Oakley Meta. Languages: 35+. Account requires no proof of disability. Volunteer side: also free, ~7M registered volunteers (2026).

## Maturity

**Production.** Used daily by hundreds of thousands of blind users worldwide. Partner companies (Microsoft, P&G, Sony, banks, airlines) pay for routing of customer-service calls. The Meta smart-glasses integration is the current frontier: hands-free, eyes-up, no phone in hand.

Gaps:
- Hallucinates occasionally on text it cannot see clearly (will guess at a pill name); users learn to verify critical info.
- Cannot read very small text reliably (sub-8pt at typical phone distance).
- Live video QA (continuous scene narration as you walk) is still less reliable than asking a single-frame question.
- No persistent memory across sessions (each interaction is a fresh context).
