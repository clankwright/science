# You.com — ARI (Advanced Research & Insights)

- **Vendor:** You.com
- **Launched:** February 27, 2025 (ARI); ARI Enterprise May 15, 2025
- **URL:** https://you.com/ari

## Positioning

Pitched as a "professional-grade research agent" targeting consultants, financial analysts, market researchers — replacing $1k-$10k boutique research reports. TIME named ARI to its 2025 Best Inventions list.

## Distinctive features

- **400+ sources per query.** Roughly 10x the source count of typical Deep Research tools. Trades per-source depth for breadth.
- **5-minute reports.** Marketed as 3x faster than OpenAI Deep Research at delivery.
- **Polished PDF output.** Reports come as formatted PDFs with charts, graphs, interactive visualizations — designed to be client-deliverable.
- **Per-report pricing.** Charges per report rather than per token / per seat — explicit alignment to "this replaces a consultant's deliverable."
- **Internal + premium data integration.** ARI Enterprise unifies public web + the customer's internal data + premium licensed datasets (e.g. financial filings) into one search surface.

## Performance claim

You.com published a benchmark of complex consultant/investment research questions where ARI Enterprise won 76% of head-to-head comparisons against OpenAI Deep Research. Vendor-published, but the methodology is described.

## Access and pricing

- Consumer: per-report pricing (currently not free; tiered subscription with report quotas).
- Enterprise: contract pricing, no public list.
- Less individual-friendly than OpenAI/Gemini/Perplexity — built for businesses.

## Weaknesses

- Lower mind-share with individuals.
- Consumer pricing less transparent.
- 400-source breadth helps coverage but encourages source-quality dilution.
