# ElevenLabs

## Vendor / access
- ElevenLabs Inc. (US, founded 2022). https://elevenlabs.io
- Web app + API. Self-serve signup, no waitlist.
- Free tier exists; paid plans start at $5/mo.

## Products
- **Text-to-Speech.** Multilingual v2, Flash, Turbo voice models. 30+ languages.
- **Instant Voice Cloning.** Upload 1-5 minutes of audio, clone voice. Available from Creator tier ($22/mo) and up.
- **Professional Voice Cloning.** 30+ minutes of audio for hyper-realistic results. Creator tier and up.
- **Conversational AI Agents.** Production voice agents with sub-second latency. Per-minute billing on top of plan minutes.
- **Dubbing Studio.** Translate + voice-clone video into 30+ languages preserving original speaker.
- **Voice Library.** Marketplace of community-shared synthetic voices.
- **Sound effects, music** (newer 2025 additions, less mature).

## Pricing (May 2026)
- Free: 10k credits/mo (~10 min TTS or ~15 min agent).
- Starter: $5/mo, ~30 min/mo.
- Creator: $22/mo, ~100 min/mo. **First tier with voice cloning.**
- Pro: $99/mo, ~500 min.
- Scale: $330/mo.
- Business: $990/mo.
- Conversational AI Agents: $0.08/min (Standard), $0.10/min (Turbo), $0.12/min (Premium) on top of plan minutes.
- Credits: 1 credit = 1 character on Multilingual v2; 0.5 credits/char on Flash/Turbo.

## Maturity
**Production.** Default in the industry. Used by audiobook publishers (Speechki, Storytel), game studios, podcasters. Sets the quality benchmark other vendors compare to.

## Distinctive trait
Only vendor that bundles cloning, TTS, agents, and dubbing in one product at consumer prices. The "Adobe of voice."

## What an individual unlocks
- Audiobook narration of any text in their own voice
- YouTube dubbing into 30+ languages
- A phone-style conversational AI for their own product or hobby
- Custom assistant voice that's not GPT's default

## Limits
- Pricing escalates fast for high-volume creators (>500 min/mo).
- Voice cloning ToS prohibits cloning without consent but enforcement is reactive.
- Watermark on output is easily stripped.
