# Specific Unlocks: 60+ Concrete Things Individuals Can Now Do

> **Purpose:** The capability pages catalog broad markets. This page catalogs the *specific* things individuals can now do that were impossible, prohibitively expensive, or required institutional access before 2023. Brainstorm-grade — items vary in maturity and overlap with existing capability pages. The goal is range, not curation.

Each entry: what is now possible, the rough pre-AI baseline, and a one-line "why this is the unlock."

## Accessibility and disability augmentation

1. **Real-time scene description for blind users.** Be My Eyes + GPT-4o, Seeing AI, Aira. Pre-AI: paid sighted-volunteer call or trained guide dog. Unlock: phone camera narrates the world continuously, reads menus, identifies pills, describes faces.
2. **Live captioning with speaker diarization on any conversation.** Apple Live Captions, Google Live Transcribe, Otter, Granola. Pre-AI: CART stenographer at $100-200/hr. Unlock: deaf/HoH users get captions on in-person speech, free.
3. **Sign language to speech, real-time.** SLAIT, SignAll, Google MediaPipe Hand Landmarker pipelines. Pre-AI: human ASL interpreter at $75-150/hr or none. Unlock: prototype-grade two-way conversation; signing → text → TTS, speech → animated avatar signing.
4. **Voice banking and restoration for ALS, stroke, laryngectomy.** ElevenLabs Voice Library, Acapela, VocaliD. Pre-AI: pre-canned synthetic voice that sounded nothing like you. Unlock: 30 seconds of recorded audio is enough to preserve your voice; restored from old voicemails for patients who never banked.
5. **Eye-gaze typing accelerated by LLM word prediction.** Tobii + Predictable + LLM. Pre-AI: 5-15 words per minute laborious selection. Unlock: full-sentence prediction triples throughput.
6. **AAC (augmentative and alternative communication) for non-speaking users.** Proloquo + LLM-driven phrase suggestion. Pre-AI: button grids requiring caregiver pre-programming. Unlock: open-vocabulary expression for autism, cerebral palsy, intellectual disability.
7. **Cognitive load reduction for ADHD/dyslexia.** ChatGPT-driven email triage, summarization, tone-translation; Speechify and Audible's AI narration of any web page. Pre-AI: executive-function failure was a personal moral problem. Unlock: outboard executive function.
8. **Pill identification and medication reconciliation by photo.** Multimodal LLMs match imprint codes against FDA NDC. Pre-AI: pharmacist callback or Drugs.com text search by code. Unlock: elderly users can verify "is this the right pill" without reading 8pt type.

## Voice, speech, and language at the long tail

9. **Conversational practice in any language with no human tutor.** Speak, Univerbal, ChatGPT advanced voice. Pre-AI: italki at $15-40/hr or no tutor available for Lithuanian, Burmese, regional Arabic dialects. Unlock: 24/7 fluent counterparty in 100+ languages.
10. **Translation in dead and endangered languages.** GPT-4 has produced plausible Sumerian, Akkadian, Linear B, Old Norse outputs; researchers fine-tune on inscription corpora. Pre-AI: 6 living scholars in the world. Unlock: amateur philology.
11. **Real-time conversation translation with mouth resync.** HeyGen Mouth Sync, Apple Live Translate. Pre-AI: human interpreter or stilted phone-based service. Unlock: family video calls across languages with the speaker's actual voice and mouth movement.
12. **Custom audiobook narration in your voice — or a deceased relative's.** ElevenLabs voice clone applied to ebook → audiobook. Pre-AI: $200-1000 of voice-actor time per book. Unlock: read your child a bedtime story in your own voice while traveling; let your kids hear grandpa narrate the family history he wrote.
13. **Music transcription from audio to tab/sheet.** Klangio, Mousai, AnthemScore. Pre-AI: hire a transcriber at $30-60 per song. Unlock: amateur musicians can learn any recorded song.
14. **Voice memos as a searchable second brain.** Whisper + RAG. Pre-AI: voice memos were a black hole. Unlock: walking-around thinking becomes queryable.
15. **Diarized transcription of family history interviews, oral histories, recorded depositions.** WhisperX, faster-whisper, pyannote. Pre-AI: $1.50-3.00 per audio minute professional transcription. Unlock: hobbyist genealogists can process hundreds of hours.

## Investigation, research, and historical work

16. **Handwritten manuscript deciphering — including cursive, court hand, secretary hand, kurrent.** Transkribus + LLM post-correction. Pre-AI: paleographer required for medieval/early-modern documents. Unlock: hobbyist genealogists read 18th-century parish registers.
17. **Genealogy in foreign-language archives.** Polish, Russian Cyrillic, Yiddish, Hebrew, Ukrainian church records → LLM transcription + translation. Pre-AI: pay a professional researcher in country at $50-200/hr. Unlock: full ancestral village reconstruction.
18. **OSINT and investigative journalism enhancement.** Bellingcat-style reverse image search, geolocation from photo background, satellite-image change detection. Pre-AI: required institutional access to Planet Labs, Maxar, professional analysts. Unlock: amateur conflict monitoring, missing-person work, scam-network mapping.
19. **Cold-case sleuthing.** Autogenealogy via DNA + LLM-assisted family-tree triangulation (Golden State Killer pattern democratized). Pre-AI: required law-enforcement access. Unlock: adoptee birth-parent searches, John/Jane Doe identifications by hobbyists.
20. **Public records and FOIA processing.** LLM extraction over scanned PDFs, automatic CSV generation. Pre-AI: hand transcription of city council minutes, court dockets, lobbying filings. Unlock: civic-tech work by individuals.
21. **Patent search and prior-art analysis.** PatentsView + Claude/GPT, Patsnap personal tier. Pre-AI: $400-2000 search by attorney/agent. Unlock: indie inventors run their own freedom-to-operate analysis.
22. **Reverse engineering of binaries and proprietary file formats.** Ghidra + LLM, IDA Pro AI assistant, decompilation summarization. Pre-AI: reserved for senior security researchers. Unlock: hobbyist game-modding, retrocomputing, IoT firmware analysis.
23. **Code archaeology of inherited COBOL, Fortran, RPG, Delphi, perl4 codebases.** GPT-4 explains and re-documents. Pre-AI: hire a contractor who actually knows COBOL ($200+/hr, retiring fast). Unlock: small businesses on legacy systems can self-serve maintenance.
24. **Old photo restoration, colorization, and animation.** MyHeritage Deep Nostalgia, Topaz Photo AI, Magnific. Pre-AI: $50-200/photo at a restoration shop. Unlock: family archives become viewable, shareable, animated for grieving.
25. **Cuneiform and ancient-script tablet reading.** Researchers (Lassner et al.) released LLM-fine-tuned models for Akkadian translation. Pre-AI: PhD specialist required. Unlock: museum and amateur access to untranslated tablets (hundreds of thousands sit unread).

## Science and technical work for amateurs

26. **Personal genomics interpretation.** Feed 23andMe / AncestryDNA raw VCF or BAM to LLM with PromethEase-style annotations. Pre-AI: $200-500 genetic counselor session covering 5-20 SNPs. Unlock: deep, ongoing exploration of one's own genome with literature grounding (and risks of misinterpretation).
27. **Amateur protein folding and ligand docking.** AlphaFold 3, RoseTTAFold All-Atom, Boltz-1, ESMFold. Pre-AI: required wet lab and crystallography. Unlock: kitchen-table structural biology for rare-disease parents trying to understand their child's variant.
28. **Math proof verification using Lean + LLM.** Terence Tao's public Lean projects, miniF2F benchmarks, AlphaProof. Pre-AI: amateur mathematicians had no way to know if their proof was correct. Unlock: formal verification at hobbyist scale.
29. **Scientific literature review at publication grade.** Elicit, Consensus, Undermind, Sci-Hub-adjacent + Claude. Pre-AI: months of manual search-and-skim per topic. Unlock: rare-disease parents, amateur researchers, journalists produce literature reviews comparable to graduate students.
30. **Personal lab notebook agent and amateur experimentation.** OpenTrons + LLM script generation, JoVE-style protocol decomposition. Pre-AI: required institutional lab. Unlock: biohackers, citizen scientists run reproducible experiments at home.
31. **Wildlife identification at species level — audio and visual.** Merlin Bird ID (free, all 10,000+ bird species), iNaturalist Seek, BirdNET-Pi backyard monitoring. Pre-AI: field guides + decades of experience. Unlock: anyone can do citizen-science quality biodiversity surveys.
32. **Plant disease and pest identification.** PlantNet, PictureThis. Pre-AI: county extension agent. Unlock: backyard gardeners get diagnosis and treatment.
33. **Mushroom identification with toxicology cross-reference.** iNaturalist + LLM follow-up — with explicit "do not eat without expert confirmation" caveats. Pre-AI: required mycology club membership. Unlock: foraging at lower (not zero) risk.
34. **Astronomy: telescope + AI for amateur exoplanet, asteroid, comet detection.** Stellina/Vespera + plate-solving + LLM-assisted analysis. Pre-AI: required observatory access. Unlock: backyard astronomers contribute discoveries (e.g., Gaia CV Hunter pipeline mentioned in this repo).
35. **Geology and rock identification by photo.** Rock Identifier, Picture Rocks. Pre-AI: geology textbook plus a hand lens. Unlock: hobbyist mineralogy.

## Hardware, fabrication, and the physical world

36. **CAD design from natural language.** Fusion 360 + Cursor/Claude generating Python API scripts; Zoo.dev (Onshape AI). Pre-AI: months learning parametric CAD. Unlock: custom fabricated parts (3D-printed brackets, laser-cut enclosures) on a single afternoon.
37. **3D printable models from text or photo.** Spline AI, Luma Genie, Meshy, NVIDIA Edify 3D. Pre-AI: Blender or Fusion 360 expertise. Unlock: print-ready STL from a description.
38. **Microcontroller firmware for hobbyists.** Arduino/ESP32/RP2040 sketches generated by Claude. Pre-AI: required understanding the language and the libraries. Unlock: home-automation, garden-monitoring, custom keyboards by non-engineers.
39. **PCB schematic and layout assistance.** Flux.ai, Quilter. Pre-AI: KiCad / Eagle expertise. Unlock: indie hardware prototypes go from idea to fab order in days.
40. **Drone and robot piloting via natural language.** Skydio waypoints + LLM, Spot + LLM scripting. Pre-AI: custom code per mission. Unlock: search-and-rescue volunteers, farmers, surveyors operate autonomous platforms with natural-language tasking.

## Game development, interactive storytelling, and creative tools

41. **Solo TTRPG game-mastering and interactive fiction.** Friends & Fables, Ai Dungeon, custom Claude-driven D&D parties. Pre-AI: needed a human GM. Unlock: solo play of GM-led tabletop RPGs.
42. **Bespoke bedtime stories starring your kids.** ChatGPT + DALL-E illustrations. Pre-AI: parental imagination at end of long day. Unlock: personalized 5-minute stories on any topic the kid asks.
43. **Game asset and game logic generation for non-coders.** Roblox Code Assist, Unity Muse, Godot + Cursor. Pre-AI: years of programming and art. Unlock: hobbyist game development; school-age kids ship playable games.
44. **Custom UI generation — disposable single-use apps.** Claude Artifacts, v0.dev, Anthropic Skills. Pre-AI: every personal data tool was a spreadsheet or didn't exist. Unlock: "build me a calorie tracker that filters by time of day" in 30 seconds.
45. **Solo full-stack indie SaaS.** Cursor + Claude Code + Stripe + Vercel + Supabase. Pre-AI: required team or contractors. Unlock: documented one-person $10k+ MRR launches in 4-8 weeks.

## Personal services at filing/clinical/professional grade

46. **Pro-se legal filings: immigration, family court, eviction defense, small claims.** Claude / GPT + court template + jurisdiction-specific guides. Pre-AI: $1500-5000 attorney retainer or self-rep with Nolo books. Unlock: filing-grade pleadings for users who couldn't afford counsel.
47. **Estate planning at low cost.** Trust & Will, FreeWill, Wealth.com — AI-assisted plus attorney review. Pre-AI: $500-3000 attorney drafting. Unlock: middle-class users actually have a will and HCPOA.
48. **Grant writing for small nonprofits and individuals.** Grantable, Instrumentl + LLM. Pre-AI: $50-150/hr grant writer. Unlock: tiny orgs apply for foundation grants they previously skipped.
49. **Insurance claim drafting and appeals.** GPT-driven appeal letters citing policy language and state insurance code. Pre-AI: 3-4 hours of letter drafting per appeal, often skipped. Unlock: medical-necessity appeals win at higher rates with LLM-assisted drafting.
50. **Tax-loss harvesting and direct indexing under $100k.** Wealthfront, Frec, Fidelity FidFolios. Pre-AI: $1M+ minimum. Unlock: TLH and direct indexing for ordinary investors.
51. **Personal CFO: holistic financial dashboard with planning conversations.** Range, Origin, Monarch + LLM advisor layer. Pre-AI: $2000-5000/yr CFP advisor. Unlock: middle-class households get planning conversations.

## Health, fitness, and longitudinal personal data

52. **Wearable data interpretation as longitudinal coaching.** Whoop Coach, Oura Advisor, Apple Health LLM (rumored), MacroFactor. Pre-AI: data dashboards no one acted on. Unlock: "your HRV dropped 12% over the past two weeks; here are the three behaviors most likely driving it."
53. **Form analysis from phone-camera squat/deadlift/swim.** Oncoach, FormCheck, OpenPose pipelines. Pre-AI: $50-200/session in-person coach. Unlock: amateur-athlete technique correction.
54. **Sleep, supplement, and nutrition stack tuning from biomarkers.** InsideTracker + LLM, Function Health + chat overlay. Pre-AI: required functional-medicine doc. Unlock: blood panel + LLM produces actionable recommendations (with the obvious caveats).
55. **Pediatric symptom triage at parent level.** OpenEvidence parent-mode prototypes, K Health Pediatrics. Pre-AI: 3am ER visit or 24-hour worry. Unlock: literature-grounded "is this an ER trip or watchful waiting" guidance.
56. **Pet health: image-based skin/eye/dental triage.** Vetster + photo, AnimalBiome. Pre-AI: $100-300 vet visit. Unlock: first-pass triage that reduces unneeded visits and surfaces real ones.

## Eldercare, neurodivergence, and the long tail

57. **Eldercare augmentation: medication reminders, fall detection, scam-call detection.** Sensi.AI, ElliQ, Apple/Google scam screen with LLM intent detection. Pre-AI: human caregiver or institutional placement. Unlock: aging-in-place feasibility extends 2-5 years.
58. **Memory aids for early-stage dementia.** Daily-routine narration, name recall via face recognition, conversation summarization. Pre-AI: spiral-notebook system. Unlock: longer cognitive independence.
59. **Education at the long tail: rural, remote, and neurodivergent learners.** Khanmigo, Synthesis Tutor, Claude Learning Mode. Pre-AI: one-size-fits-all classroom or expensive specialist. Unlock: 2-sigma-grade tutoring for kids in places without it; ND-tailored pacing.
60. **Solo caregiver decision support for rare-disease parents.** Literature synthesis + variant interpretation + protocol comparison. Pre-AI: required academic medical center access. Unlock: parents arrive at appointments with peer-reviewed-grounded questions.

## Sovereignty, ownership, and local-first AI

61. **Local-first agentic AI: Ollama + Continue + open-source SWE agents on a laptop.** Pre-AI: nothing comparable. Unlock: censorship-free, offline, private inference; runs in airgapped or sensitive environments.
62. **Local image generation: ComfyUI, Forge, SDXL/Flux on a 24GB GPU.** Pre-AI: gated by hosted-API content policies. Unlock: artists, researchers, adult creators, journalists in restrictive regions own the full pipeline.
63. **MCP server ecosystem as connective tissue.** Composio, Anthropic-built servers, hundreds of community servers. Pre-AI: agents had no standard way to touch your tools. Unlock: agents that drive your Gmail, Calendar, GitHub, Notion, bank without per-app integrations.
64. **Personal RAG over your own corpus.** LlamaIndex/LangChain personal projects, NotebookLM, Karpathy LLM-wiki pattern. Pre-AI: knowledge management was filing systems. Unlock: every document you've ever owned becomes conversationally addressable.
65. **Owning your AI-generated audio/video persona.** Banked voice, banked likeness, banked writing samples → your "agent" can act on your behalf for years. Pre-AI: would have died with you. Unlock: cross-generational personal AI inheritance — and the obvious ethical/legal mess.
