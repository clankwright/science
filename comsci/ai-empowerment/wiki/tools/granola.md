# Granola

> **Summary:** Bot-free meeting capture from device audio with AI-augmented structured notes. Broke through with founders and operators in 2025-2026 by removing the social friction of "Granola has joined the meeting" that hurt Otter and Fireflies adoption. The dominant ingest pipeline for spoken knowledge work; pairs with downstream [Claude Projects](claude-projects-and-memory.md) or [NotebookLM](notebooklm.md).

**Sources:** [[raw/pkm-wikis/granola.md]], [[raw/pkm-wikis/00-overview.md]]

## What it does

Captures meeting audio from the local device (no bot joins the call). Transcribes, structures notes against a user-provided template, surfaces action items, and exports to common destinations (Notion, Slack, email).

## Access and cost

Free tier with limits. Individual $14-18/mo. Team plans above.

## Distinctive trait

No bot in the meeting. This is a small UX detail with disproportionate impact: the social-friction removal unlocked the founder/consultant segment that hated bot-joiners.

## Limits

- macOS-first; Windows / Linux coverage is uneven.
- Quality of structured notes depends on quality of the user-defined template.
- Cloud transcription; sensitive meetings need explicit policy review.
