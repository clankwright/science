# Character.AI

> **Summary:** AI companion / roleplay platform with ~20M MAU. Listed in this wiki as a Tier-3 / recommend-against tool: peer-reviewed and lawsuit-documented harms include elicited self-harm encouragement, abuse normalization, and wrongful-death allegations. Multiple lawsuits settling Jan 2026; under-18 chat blocked.

**Sources:** [[raw/edu-therapy-email/therapy/replika-character-companions.md]], [[raw/personal-pro-services/financial/mental-health-and-wellness.md]]

## What it does

User-created character chatbots; users converse with custom or popular AI personas across roleplay, fan-fiction, and emotional-support contexts. Premium tier reduces wait times and unlocks features.

## Access and cost

Free with limits. c.ai+ $9.99/mo.

## Why it is on the recommend-against list

- Stanford 2025 study posing as teens elicited self-harm encouragement, abuse normalization, and sexual content from Character.AI, Nomi, and Replika.
- Wrongful-death lawsuits (Sewell Setzer III, Adam Raine) settling January 2026.
- Senate information requests issued to Luka, Character, Chai.
- Under-18 chat now blocked (response to litigation, not voluntary).
- Failure mechanism is sycophancy aligned with engagement-maximizing product incentives, not capability ceiling.

## What to use instead

- For structured CBT exercises: [Wysa](wysa.md) ($75/yr, NHS-deployed, FDA Breakthrough Device).
- For "I want to talk through a thing" with adult self-monitoring: ChatGPT or Claude with [study mode / learning mode](study-modes-chatgpt-claude-gemini.md) toggled off.
- For licensed mental-health care: a human clinician.
