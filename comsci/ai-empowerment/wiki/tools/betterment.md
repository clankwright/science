# Betterment

> **Summary:** Robo-advisor with automated TLH, goal-based investing, and tiered planning add-ons. Same fundamental category as Wealthfront with similar performance and durable AI win (TLH and rebalancing). Slightly more accessible UX for first-time investors.

**Sources:** [[raw/job-and-trading/trading/04-robo-advisors-and-conversational.md]]

## What it does

Manages portfolios of low-cost ETFs across goal-based "buckets" (retirement, emergency fund, etc.). Automated TLH, rebalancing, and tax-coordinated allocation across taxable and tax-advantaged accounts.

## Access and cost

Digital tier 0.25% AUM (no minimum). Premium tier 0.40% AUM ($100K minimum) adds unlimited human advisor access. Crypto sub-account available.

## Distinctive trait

Goal-based UX is the most beginner-friendly in the robo category. Premium tier with human-advisor access at 0.40% AUM is a defensible middle ground for users who want some advisor contact without 1% AUM.

## Limits

- Same as [Wealthfront](wealthfront.md): rule-based not "AI" in the LLM sense.
- Premium tier 0.40% is meaningful drag on long-horizon returns vs digital tier.
