# Simplify Copilot

> **Summary:** Browser-extension job-application autofill tool. 1M+ installs, covers 100+ ATS platforms. The durable category leader for assistive-mode job application: the user stays in the submit loop, AI handles the form-filling tedium. Pair with [Rezi](rezi-resume.md) or Teal for tailored resume + cover letter, then submit by hand.

**Sources:** [[raw/job-and-trading/job-applications/02-simplify-and-autofill.md]], [[raw/job-and-trading/job-applications/00-overview.md]]

## What it does

Browser extension that autofills application forms across 100+ ATS platforms (Workday, Greenhouse, Lever, Taleo, etc.) from a stored profile. Tracks applications, suggests jobs, and integrates with resume tailoring.

## Access and cost

Core extension free. Pro tier adds advanced tracking, multiple resume profiles, application analytics.

## Distinctive trait

Free + works across the long tail of ATS platforms. Defensible value: removes the 20-40 minutes per Workday-style form without the recruiter-side ATS-filter problem that catches volume blasters.

## Limits

- Still requires the user to click submit (this is the feature, not a bug).
- Profile maintenance is on the user.
- Quality of resume / cover letter still depends on the underlying materials; pair with [Rezi](rezi-resume.md) for tailoring.
