# Khanmigo

> **Summary:** Khan Academy's Socratic-constrained AI tutor, scoped to the Khan Academy curriculum. ~1M students. Microsoft-funded scale, multilingual through 2025. The default K-12 curriculum-grounded tutor at $4/mo for learners and free for teachers.

**Sources:** [[raw/edu-therapy-email/education/khanmigo.md]]

## What it does

Conversational tutor inside Khan Academy. Refuses to give direct answers; asks Socratic questions; walks through derivations; offers hints calibrated to the user's current level. Covers the same K-12 + AP + intro-college topics as Khan Academy's video curriculum.

## Access and cost

$4/mo for learners. Free for teachers (Khan-verified). Free for Khan Academy donor-funded users in some districts.

## Distinctive trait

Curriculum-grounding is the differentiator. Generic chat-app study modes are unconstrained; Khanmigo is wired to a specific course and pedagogy, making it more pedagogically effective for learners working through Khan curriculum.

## Limits

- Constrained to Khan Academy curriculum; not useful for arbitrary topics.
- K-12 + intro-college focus; no graduate or professional content.
- Less general than [study modes](study-modes-chatgpt-claude-gemini.md).
