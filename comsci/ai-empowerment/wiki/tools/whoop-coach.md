# Whoop Coach

> **Summary:** LLM overlay (originally GPT-4) on top of Whoop's strap and longitudinal data. Conversational queries against your training load, sleep, recovery, and journaled habits. Behavior Insights does automatic n=1 statistics on journaled habits once you have 5 yes / 5 no datapoints in a 90-day window: quietly the most rigorous AI-coach feature in the category.

**Sources:** [[raw/wearable-coaching/whoop-coach.md]]

## What it does

Ask questions ("why did my readiness drop this week?", "what's the longest I've held a high HRV trend?") and get synthesized answers from your data. Behavior Insights surfaces automatic correlations between journaled habits (alcohol, late meals, magnesium) and recovery metrics, with effect sizes and significance flags.

## Access and cost

Membership-based: $30/mo or $239/yr. Hardware (the strap) is bundled with active membership. Coach included in all tiers.

## Distinctive trait

Behavior Insights. Most wearable AI overlays are chat with no statistical machinery; Whoop's habit-vs-recovery analysis is closer to actual personal-experiment infrastructure. The strap also wins on continuous wear (no display, no charging gap with the wireless battery pack).

## Limits

- Wrist HRV is noisier than chest-strap HRV; coaching only as good as the underlying signal.
- Conversational responses sometimes hallucinate specifics that aren't in your data.
- No display means no on-device feedback; phone-tethered.
- 30/mo subscription is steep relative to one-time competitors.
