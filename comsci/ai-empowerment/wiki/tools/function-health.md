# Function Health

> **Summary:** $499/yr for ~100 biomarkers across two panels per year, with LLM-driven explanations and protocol suggestions. Made longitudinal blood panels a "coachable corpus" rather than 15 numbers in a portal. Competitors include Superpower, Inside Tracker, and Marek Health.

**Sources:** [[raw/wearable-coaching/function-health-and-blood-panels.md]]

## What it does

Two annual blood draws, ~100 biomarkers each (lipid panel, advanced lipids ApoB and Lp(a), inflammation, hormones, thyroid, metabolic, vitamins, heavy metals, more). LLM explains each marker, flags out-of-range values, and suggests next steps.

## Access and cost

$499/yr membership. Includes labs and the digital interface. Optional add-ons (advanced cancer markers, heart MRI) at extra cost.

## Distinctive trait

The bundling of comprehensive panels with a readable, conversational interface. The comparable list at LabCorp or Quest direct-pay is similar in cost but produces an unreadable PDF; Function makes the data the artifact and the coach the medium. Co-founded by Mark Hyman; functional-medicine framing influences recommendations.

## Limits

- Many of the markers in the panel have weak or no clinical evidence at population level; surfacing them risks anxiety and unnecessary follow-up.
- LLM "protocol suggestions" sometimes recommend supplements without strong clinical backing.
- Not a substitute for an annual physical with a primary-care physician.
- Dietitian/clinician chat (in higher tiers) carries most of the actual value; the AI overlay alone is less differentiated.
