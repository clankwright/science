# Perplexity Computer (incl. tax-return drafting)

> **Summary:** Perplexity's agentic "Computer" mode performs multi-step tasks over a user's documents. Its tax workflow ("Navigate my taxes", 2026 filing season) reviews uploaded financial documents, asks follow-up questions, maps inputs to official IRS forms, and drafts a federal return, or audits a professionally prepared one. Grounded in continuously updated IRS modules, not static training data.

**Sources:** [[raw/2026-06-update/perplexity-computer-taxes.md]]

## What it does

Upload financial documents; the agent interviews the user, maps inputs to live IRS forms, and produces a drafted federal return. It can also check an accountant's return for errors. The IRS-module grounding addresses the staleness problem of LLMs reasoning about tax from training data.

## Access and cost

Perplexity Pro ($17-20/mo). US federal returns. This is a new capability on the [Perplexity](perplexity.md) subscription already covered for research, not a separate product.

## What changed

AI tax help was previously generic Q&A. This drafts an actual filled return against current IRS forms, work that meant a TurboTax wizard or a $200-500 paid preparer.

## Individual empowerment

An individual can have an AI prepare an actual federal return, or independently check a preparer's work, for the price of a Pro subscription rather than a preparer fee.

## Limits

- Preview quality; a drafted return still needs human review before filing.
- US federal only; state returns and complex situations (business, multi-state) are weaker.
- Uploading full financial records to a third party carries the usual data-exposure risk.
