# Rezi

> **Summary:** AI resume builder with ATS-optimized formatting and per-posting tailoring. 4.3M users. The strongest defensible value-add in the automated-job-application category alongside [Simplify Copilot](simplify-copilot.md).

**Sources:** [[raw/job-and-trading/job-applications/04-resume-and-interview.md]]

## What it does

Builds and tailors resumes for ATS readability. Per-posting tailoring suggests keywords from the job description, rewrites bullets to match required experience phrasing, and exports clean formats.

## Access and cost

Free tier (limited). Pro $29/mo or $129/yr. Lifetime tier above.

## Distinctive trait

ATS-optimization focus is the actual value: resumes parsed cleanly by the systems recruiters use. Per-posting tailoring is materially better than generic AI cover-letter generators because it stays close to the source posting.

## Limits

- Tailoring is only as good as the underlying resume; thin resumes do not become strong via formatting.
- Subscription cost adds up for users not actively job-searching; pause/resume is reasonable.
