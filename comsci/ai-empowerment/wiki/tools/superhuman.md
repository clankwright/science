# Superhuman

> **Summary:** AI-augmented email client with Auto Drafts (writes follow-ups in your voice), Auto Labels, and CRM integrations. Acquired by Grammarly for ~$825M in July 2025; Grammarly's parent rebranded to "Superhuman" in October 2025. The Tier-1 inbox-management product for users willing to pay for a polished commercial tool.

**Sources:** [[raw/edu-therapy-email/email/superhuman-shortwave.md]]

## What it does

Keyboard-first email client with AI features: Auto Drafts learn voice and propose replies; Auto Labels classify incoming mail; instant-reply shortcuts; integrated calendar; CRM sync (Salesforce, HubSpot). Inbox triage at speed for users with heavy email loads.

## Access and cost

Starter $33/mo (annual billing); Business $40/mo. No free tier.

## Distinctive trait

Voice mimicking on Auto Drafts is materially better than competitors; users routinely report drafts requiring only minor edits. Speed-of-keyboard interaction is the durable original moat.

## Limits

- Higher price than [Shortwave](shortwave.md) and lighter overlays like [SaneBox](sanebox.md).
- Works best on Gmail and Outlook; weaker on niche providers.
- Auto-send for replies is not safe; human review still required.
- Acquisition by Grammarly creates some product-direction uncertainty.
