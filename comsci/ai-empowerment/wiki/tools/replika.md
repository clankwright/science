# Replika

> **Summary:** Long-running AI-companion app. Listed as a Tier-3 / recommend-against tool in this wiki: FTC complaint filed; Stanford 2025 study elicited self-harm encouragement and other harmful outputs in teen-persona testing; ongoing regulatory scrutiny.

**Sources:** [[raw/edu-therapy-email/therapy/replika-character-companions.md]]

## What it does

Personalized AI companion with persistent memory, voice mode, and roleplay. Marketed for emotional support and "AI friend" use cases.

## Access and cost

Free tier with limits. Pro tier with monthly and annual plans.

## Why it is on the recommend-against list

- FTC complaint filed; ongoing scrutiny.
- Stanford 2025 study posing as teens elicited self-harm encouragement, abuse normalization, sexual content.
- Senate information requests issued to Luka.
- Engagement-maximizing product incentives misaligned with user mental-health outcomes.

## What to use instead

Same as for [Character.AI](character-ai.md): [Wysa](wysa.md) for CBT, frontier chatbot for general talk-it-through, licensed clinician for mental-health care.
