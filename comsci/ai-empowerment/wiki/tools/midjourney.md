# Midjourney

> **Summary:** Aesthetic-default text-to-image product. v7 / v8 (2025-2026) compete with FLUX, Imagen 4, and gpt-image-1 on quality; Midjourney's edge is specifically the painterly / cinematic aesthetic baked into the default model. The most-used commercial image generator for solo creators in 2026.

**Sources:** [[raw/generative-media/image/midjourney.md]], [[raw/generative-media/capability-overview.md]]

## What it does

Generates images from text prompts or image+text. Strong in cinematic composition, illustration, concept art, and stylized photography. Web UI plus the original Discord bot interface.

## Access and cost

Basic $10/mo, Standard $30/mo (unlimited Relax mode), Pro $60/mo, Mega $120/mo. No free tier as of May 2026.

## Distinctive trait

Aesthetic baked into the default model. Comparable raw quality to FLUX or Imagen 4, but Midjourney outputs look "Midjourney" in a way the others do not, which is the feature for many users.

## Limits

- Compositional control is iffy beyond hands and text; multi-character interactions still need many regenerations.
- Less instructed-edit capable than [gpt-image-1](openai-gpt-image.md).
- Closed-API; no self-hostable equivalent.
- Detectable "AI look" in some commercial contexts.
