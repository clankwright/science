# Eight Sleep Pod

> **Summary:** Heated and cooled mattress cover with sleep tracking. Pod 4 plus Autopilot 4.0 (2024) is the only consumer product that closes the loop: senses sleep stage, adjusts thermal environment in real time, re-senses. Everything else in the category just narrates.

**Sources:** [[raw/wearable-coaching/sleep-coaching-and-rise-eight.md]]

## What it does

Active heating and cooling per side, real-time temperature adjustment based on sensed sleep stage and heart rate. Tracks sleep stages, HRV, RHR, snoring. Vibration alarms. Works with HealthKit and Health Connect.

## Access and cost

Pod 4 from $2,295 plus Pod cover and base. Required Autopilot subscription $25/mo (Pro tier). Existing mattress required (the Pod is a cover, not a mattress).

## Distinctive trait

Closed-loop. Sleep monitoring without intervention is a dashboard. Eight Sleep is the only consumer product that intervenes (thermal) based on sensed state. The intervention is well-supported by sleep literature: cooler core temperature aids deep sleep, warmer aids initial sleep onset.

## Limits

- Subscription required for full Autopilot features; without it the hardware is much less useful.
- Setup requires plumbing for the water-based thermal system; some users dislike the noise.
- Reliability complaints in older units; warranty support is mixed.
- Privacy: the most intimate sensor data (body temperature, sleep, HRV) lives on Eight Sleep servers.
