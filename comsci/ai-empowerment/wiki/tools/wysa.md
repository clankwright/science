# Wysa

> **Summary:** NHS-deployed CBT chatbot with FDA Breakthrough Device designation. Dose-response evidence: twice-weekly users get >5-point PHQ-9 reductions. The strongest safety record and clinical evidence base in the consumer AI mental-health category as of May 2026, after Woebot's consumer-app shutdown in 2024.

**Sources:** [[raw/edu-therapy-email/therapy/woebot-wysa.md]]

## What it does

Structured CBT exercises in chatbot form: thought-challenging, mood tracking, sleep hygiene, anxiety management. Anonymous, 24/7, in 10+ languages. NHS-deployed across multiple trusts; offered through some employer EAPs.

## Access and cost

Free tier with core CBT exercises. Premium $74.99/yr unlocks the full library, human-coach access, and longer sessions. Some users access through NHS or employer EAP at no personal cost.

## Distinctive trait

Only consumer-facing chatbot in the category with FDA Breakthrough Device designation and published dose-response symptom reduction. NHS deployment is a meaningful safety and efficacy signal that no companion app shares.

## Limits

- Narrow scope: works for mild-to-moderate symptoms; not a substitute for a clinician for crisis or severe presentations.
- Long-term efficacy beyond 2-3 months is under-studied (true of all AI mental-health products).
- Crisis detection exists but a chatbot cannot fully substitute for human escalation.
