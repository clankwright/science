# Oura Advisor

> **Summary:** LLM overlay on Oura Ring 4 sensor data. The longitudinal-sleep coach on what is generally considered the strongest consumer sleep sensor. Beta 2024, general availability 2025. Stronger sleep coverage than Whoop, weaker training-load coverage.

**Sources:** [[raw/wearable-coaching/oura-advisor.md]]

## What it does

Conversational interface over Oura's sleep, readiness, heart rate, HRV, body temperature, and activity data. Trend synthesis ("how has my baseline body temperature shifted across the past three months?"), behavior reflection, and goal coaching.

## Access and cost

Oura Ring 4 hardware $349-499. Membership $5.99/mo or $69.99/yr. Advisor included in membership.

## Distinctive trait

Body-temperature trend tracking is unmatched among consumer wearables and matters for menstrual-cycle tracking, illness-onset detection, and circadian-rhythm analysis. The ring form factor wins long-term wear vs. wrist devices; sleep data quality benefits.

## Limits

- Activity tracking weaker than wrist devices; expect to pair with another tracker for serious training.
- Ring sizing is permanent; resizing requires a new ring.
- Conversational responses sometimes lean on generic wellness advice over your actual data.
