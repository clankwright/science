# MyHeritage

> **Summary:** Genealogy platform with the strongest consumer AI photo features: AI Record Finder, Deep Nostalgia animation (119M+ animations served), DeepStory, photo enhancement and colorization. Less comprehensive than FamilySearch on raw record coverage, but the AI photo layer is unmatched.

**Sources:** [[raw/investigation-osint-genealogy/genealogy-foreign-archives.md]], [[raw/investigation-osint-genealogy/old-photo-restoration.md]]

## What it does

Searches a large genealogical record set (records, trees, DNA matches). AI Record Finder surfaces likely record matches for a profile. Deep Nostalgia animates a still photo of a face with a short head-motion clip. DeepStory generates a narrated retrospective. Photo enhancer upscales and colorizes old photos.

## Access and cost

Free tier with 250 photo enhancements; Complete plan $299/yr; Premium plan $129/yr. DNA test $89.

## Distinctive trait

Photo animation. Deep Nostalgia (released 2021, refined through 2026) brought a still grandparent's face into motion: 119M+ animations as of recent reporting. The emotional resonance for users far exceeds anything in the structured-data side of genealogy. Photo enhancement and colorization are similarly polished.

## Limits

- Record coverage weaker than FamilySearch in many regions; pair the two.
- Animation quality drops on damaged photos and side profiles.
- Subscription auto-renew defaults are aggressive; cancel before renewal date.
