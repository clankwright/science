# Study modes (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini)

> **Summary:** Single-click toggles in the major chat products that flip the model into a Socratic-tutor stance: ask questions back, refuse to give direct answers on homework, walk through reasoning step by step. All three launched July-August 2025, shipping the personalized-tutor unhobbling to existing 100M+ user bases overnight.

**Sources:** [[raw/edu-therapy-email/education/chatgpt-claude-study-modes.md]], [[raw/edu-therapy-email/education/ai-tutoring-evidence.md]]

## What it does

Toggle in the chat interface; the model adopts Socratic constraints (does not directly answer homework; asks clarifying and probing questions; walks through derivations). Works across STEM, humanities, language learning, professional reskilling, and arbitrary topics within the model's training coverage.

## Access and cost

Free on Gemini and ChatGPT (free-tier limits apply); bundled with Plus / Pro tiers ($20/mo) on all three. Claude Learning Mode requires Claude Pro.

## Distinctive trait

Highest-reach educational AI tool of 2025-2026 because it shipped on top of the existing 100M+ user bases of the three major chat apps. No new account or subscription needed for users already on Plus/Pro tier.

## Limits

- Socratic constraint can be defeated by a determined user (asking variations, requesting "just give me the answer").
- Depth ceiling at advanced levels (graduate research, professional licensure) without expert verification.
- 2025 meta-analyses show g~0.5 short-term effect; long-term effect collapses to g~0.08 (novelty decay plausible). No full-school-year RCT yet.
