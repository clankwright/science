# Wealthfront

> **Summary:** Robo-advisor offering automated portfolio management with tax-loss harvesting, direct indexing (≥$100K taxable accounts), and goal-based planning via the Path planner. Predates the LLM wave but qualifies for this wiki because tax-loss harvesting + direct indexing is the only well-evidenced AI personal-finance win for individuals: 0.5-1.5%/yr durable tax alpha for high-bracket investors.

**Sources:** [[raw/job-and-trading/trading/04-robo-advisors-and-conversational.md]], [[raw/personal-pro-services/financial/range-origin-magnifi.md]]

## What it does

Manages an investment portfolio of low-cost ETFs (or, ≥$100K, individual stocks via direct indexing) according to risk tolerance. Automatically harvests realized losses for tax purposes year-round, rebalances, and re-optimizes. Path planner answers life-financial-planning questions (retirement, home purchase, college).

## Access and cost

0.25% AUM, no account minimum for the basic robo product (direct indexing requires $100K+).

## Distinctive trait

Most aggressive TLH implementation among major robo-advisors. Direct indexing at $100K threshold (lower than competitors). Decade of operating history.

## Limits

- 0.25% AUM is real money on large balances; for very large taxable accounts, customized direct-indexing services from major brokerages may have lower all-in costs.
- "AI" branding aside, this is rule-based optimization (excellent rule-based optimization).
- Does not handle complex situations (concentrated stock, K-1s, business income) well; HNW users need a human advisor or [Range](range-financial.md).
