# HeyGen

> **Summary:** Photoreal talking-head avatar generator and multilingual video dubbing tool. Avatar IV is the current realism leader for talking-head shots. Dubbing covers 175+ languages with speaker-voice preservation and lip-sync re-timing. Replaces $5,000-50,000 in dubbing-studio fees per video for solo creators going multilingual.

**Sources:** [[raw/generative-media/avatars/heygen-synthesia-hedra.md]], [[raw/voice-cloning/dubbing-heygen-rask.md]]

## What it does

Two main products: (1) Avatar IV: type a script, an AI avatar (a stock avatar or a personal avatar trained from your video) speaks it on camera with synced lip-sync. (2) Translate: upload a video, get back versions in 175+ languages with the original speaker's voice cloned and lip-sync re-timed.

## Access and cost

Free tier (3 min/mo, watermarked). Creator $29/mo. Team $89/mo. Enterprise above.

## Distinctive trait

Best-in-class realism for talking-head avatars in May 2026. Multilingual dubbing with lip-sync preservation is the killer-app for solo creators reaching multiple language markets.

## Limits

- Full-body realism still leading-edge.
- Long-tail languages have weaker lip-sync transfer than top-30.
- Consent enforcement on personal-avatar creation is attestation-only.
- Detectable avatar look on careful inspection.
