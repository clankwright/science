# AAV9-s-KL gene therapy extends lifespan ~20% in wild-type mice

## Summary
Single AAV9 injection (intracerebroventricular plus intravenous)
expressing the **secreted Klotho isoform (s-KL)** into wild-type
C57BL/6J mice at 12 months of age extended **total longevity by 19.7%**
(median +15%) in males. Treatment also rescued muscle, bone, and brain
phenotypes of aging. First demonstration of s-KL gene therapy lifespan
extension in healthy wild-type mice (no progeria background).

## Method
- AAV9 vector with CAG-driven secreted Klotho isoform (s-KL: KL1 domain
  plus 14-aa C-terminal tail).
- Combined ICV (1e11 vg) and IV (4e11 vg) injection.
- Three groups: null control at 6 months, s-KL at 6 months, s-KL at 12
  months. Followed to natural death.
- Female longevity confounded by ulcerative dermatitis and anal bleeding
  across all groups; lifespan curves only reportable for males.

## Key findings
- **+19.7% total longevity** in 12-month-treated males (p=0.005,
  Mantel-Cox); +15% median.
- 12-MO group reached higher serum s-KL than 6-MO group, suggesting
  dose-response rather than treatment-window effect.
- Reduced muscle fibrosis, larger soleus fibres, improved grip and
  rotarod performance.
- Better trabecular bone microstructure (volume, thickness, spacing),
  with elevated Bglap, Col1a1, Runx2 and reduced Fgf23 expression
  (osteoporosis-protective signature, strongest in females).
- Hippocampus: more DCX+ maturing neurons, more GFAP+/Iba1+ glia, and
  transcriptomic shift toward microglial phagocytosis and IL-10 (anti-
  inflammatory) signalling. Aged-aging hallmarks (oxidative
  phosphorylation drop, mitochondrial dysfunction) absent in s-KL group.

## Why it matters
Klotho gene therapy is an entirely separate modality from the
reprogramming/senolytic axes that dominate the field. The **secreted
isoform avoids the FGF23-pathway safety concerns of full-length p-KL**.
Translates the long-standing klotho/aging literature into a single-shot
delivery candidate. Underpins the Klotho Neurosciences (formerly
Annexon) clinical pipeline.

## Caveats
- Male-only longevity readout.
- Two-route injection (ICV + IV); IV-only delivery would need a BBB-
  crossing AAV serotype to translate.
- Combination of CAG promoter plus AAV9 means liver transduction loss
  over time; the 12-month protocol benefits from less elapsed
  cell-renewal-induced loss.
