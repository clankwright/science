# TPE+IVIG cuts biological age by 2.6 years in healthy adults (RCT)

## Summary
Buck Institute single-blinded **randomised placebo-controlled trial**.
**42 healthy adults >50 yo** randomised into three TPE protocols and a
sham arm: **biweekly TPE alone, biweekly TPE + IVIG, monthly TPE,
sham**. Biweekly TPE+IVIG produced the **largest biological-age
reduction (-2.61 years)**; biweekly TPE alone -1.32 years; monthly TPE
intermediate. Multi-omic readout (15 epigenetic clocks, immune
phenotyping). One mild allergic reaction across 240 procedures.

## Why it matters
Most rigorous human trial to date showing **plasma-exchange-mediated
biological-age reduction**. Direct counterweight to the negative result
in [[papers/plasmapheresis-aging-trial-2025]] — read together they
suggest the **active ingredient is replacement-fluid composition (IVIG)**
rather than removal of "old" plasma factors per se. Functionally
distinct from infusion-based parabiosis approaches like
[[papers/pedf-parabiosis-2024]] and [[papers/fgf17-young-csf-2022]].

## Key findings
- Biweekly TPE+IVIG: **-2.61 years** biological age vs sham.
- Biweekly TPE alone: **-1.32 years** biological age vs sham.
- Monthly TPE: intermediate effect.
- 15 epigenetic clocks all moved in the rejuvenation direction;
  reversal of immunosenescence markers (more naive CD4+/CD8+, more
  stem-cell memory T cells).
- Effect strongest in subjects with worst baseline metabolic profile.
- Long-term safety: 1 mild allergic reaction in 240 procedures.

## Caveats
- Healthy-volunteer cohort (not patients); generalisability unclear.
- IVIG is itself a complex biologic; the active fraction within IVIG
  is not yet identified.
- Single-blinded (not double).
