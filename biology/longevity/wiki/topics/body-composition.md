# Body composition

What you weigh matters less than how that weight is distributed.
Visceral and ectopic (liver, muscle) fat drive metabolic aging;
abdominal adiposity predicts mortality independently of BMI.

## Flagship evidence
- [[papers/pischon-2008-waist-mortality]] — EPIC, n=359,387.
  Waist circumference and waist-to-hip ratio independently
  predicted mortality across all BMI strata. Lowest-risk BMI
  was 25.3 (M) / 24.3 (F).

## Practical
- Measure: tape at level of natural waist (top of iliac crest /
  navel), at end of normal exhale.
- Targets: men <94 cm, women <80 cm. Intervention warranted above
  102 / 88 cm.
- DEXA scan (~$50-150) gives the higher-resolution visceral fat
  signal.
- Diet adherence beats diet selection; the
  [[papers/lifestyle-atlas-tirolgesund-2025]] data show
  intermittent-fasting compliance is the limiting factor.
