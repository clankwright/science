# Cardiorespiratory fitness (VO2max)

The maximum amount of oxygen the body can use during all-out
exercise (mL O2 / kg / min). One of the strongest single mortality
predictors in adults; effect size larger than smoking, diabetes, or
prior CAD in head-to-head analyses.

## Flagship evidence
- [[papers/mandsager-2018-vo2max-mortality]] — Cleveland Clinic,
  n=122,007. Low vs elite CRF: HR 5.04 for all-cause mortality.
  No upper limit of benefit.

## How to raise it
- [[papers/helgerud-2007-4x4-vo2max]] — Norwegian 4x4 protocol:
  4 min × 4 intervals at 90-95% HRmax, 3 min easy between. +7.2%
  VO2max in 8 weeks in moderately trained subjects; no significant
  change in continuous-moderate group.
