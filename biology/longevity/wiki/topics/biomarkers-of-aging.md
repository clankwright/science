# Biomarkers of aging

Measurable quantities (proteomic, metabolomic, epigenetic,
transcriptomic, phenotypic) that track or predict biological aging.

## Landmark resources
- [[papers/biomarkers-aging-challenge]] — active competition portal
- [[papers/nunez2024-open-competition]] — dataset + paper describing
  phases 1–2

## Large cohort studies
- [[papers/ukb-nmr-metabolomic-2024]] — 250,341 UKB participants,
  325 NMR biomarkers, 54-biomarker aging signature
- [[papers/organ-proteomic-clocks-2025]] — 43,616 UKB participants,
  organ-specific proteomic clocks
- [[papers/plasma-proteomics-brain-immune-2025]] — parallel UKB
  analysis (44,498 participants); brain + immune ages dominate.

## Foundation-model biomarker work
- [[papers/longevity-llm-2026]] — LLM that predicts age from
  multi-omic biomarkers.
- [[papers/longevity-bench-2026]] — benchmark for biomarker-to-age
  reasoning.
