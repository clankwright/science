# Brain rejuvenation

Aging-reversal work specifically targeting the central nervous system —
cognition, oligodendrogenesis, hippocampal function.

- [[papers/fgf17-young-csf-2022]] — young CSF / FGF17 rejuvenates aged
  hippocampus
- [[papers/senolytic-mci-ebiomed-2025]] — STAMINA senolytic pilot in
  MCI
- [[papers/organ-proteomic-clocks-2025]] — brain-aging proteomic clock
  is the strongest mortality predictor

- [[papers/klotho-skl-aav-2025]] — AAV9-s-KL increases hippocampal
  neurogenesis and microglial phagocytosis; +20% lifespan
- [[papers/gpld1-tnap-brain-vasculature-2026]] — exerkine-vascular
  axis; TNAP inhibition rescues memory in aging and AD models
- [[papers/ipsc-mononuclear-phagocyte-aging-brain-2025]] — IV iPSC-

- [[papers/spatial-aging-clocks-brain-2024]] — spatial brain aging
  clock; T-cell shadow vs. NSC halo proximity effects
