# Stress and Healing Speed

> **Summary:** Psychological stress measurably slows biological healing via neuroimmune pathways. The Kiecolt-Glaser wound healing result (40% slower under stress) is one of the best-controlled findings in psychoneuroimmunology. Childhood trauma (ACE study) predicts virtually every major cause of adult mortality with dose-response.

## Tier 1 Evidence

**Wound healing under stress — two studies, same mechanism:**
- [Kiecolt-Glaser 1995](../papers/kiecolt-glaser-1995.md) (Lancet, N=13 caregivers + 13 controls): Alzheimer's caregivers showed measurably impaired wound healing vs matched controls. Mechanism: reduced IL-1β at wound site.
- [Marucha 1998](../papers/marucha-1998.md) (Psychosom Med, crossover): dental students' wounds healed **40% longer** during exam period vs summer vacation. Same subjects = strongest causal design.

**Surgical prehabilitation.** [UCLA 2025](../papers/ucla-prehab-2025.md) meta-analysis (20 RCTs, N=2,376): psychological prehabilitation before surgery → 1.62 days shorter hospital stay. Effect holds across surgery types.

**Trauma and all-cause mortality.** [ACE Study 1998](../papers/felitti-1998.md) (N=9,508, CDC): Adverse Childhood Experiences show dose-response relationship with all major causes of adult death — heart disease, cancer, stroke, diabetes, COPD, liver disease. 4+ ACEs roughly doubles risk of most outcomes.

**Forest bathing and cortisol.** [Li 2009](../papers/li-2009.md): 24-forest field experiments, cortisol -13-16% in forest vs urban conditions. NK cell activity increase sustained 30 days post-visit.

## Tier 2 Evidence

**CBT + physical therapy.** [PMC 2022](../papers/cbt-physio-2022.md): multiple meta-analyses consistently show combined CBT + physical therapy outperforms physical therapy alone for musculoskeletal and chronic pain conditions.

**Breathing and parasympathetic activation.** [Komori 2018](../papers/komori-breathing-2018.md): extended-exhale breathing (exhale > inhale) reliably activates parasympathetic nervous system via vagal pathways. Diaphragmatic breathing → cortisol reduction (Hopper 2019 systematic review).

## Psychoneuroimmunology Mechanism

Stress activates HPA axis → cortisol → immune suppression → reduced cytokine production → slower wound healing and infection clearance. Simultaneously: sympathetic activation → vasoconstriction → reduced blood flow to healing tissue.

The healing environment research operationalizes this: if you can reduce psychological stress through the built environment, you are directly accelerating biological healing via the same pathway.
