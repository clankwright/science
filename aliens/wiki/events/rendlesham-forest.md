# Rendlesham Forest Incident (1980)

> **Summary:** The Rendlesham Forest incident occurred in late December 1980 near RAF Woodbridge in Suffolk, England, when U.S. Air Force personnel reported encounters with anomalous lights and a craft-like object in the adjacent forest over multiple nights. It is often called "Britain's [Roswell](roswell.md)." ## Overview

Over the nights of December 26-28, 1980, multiple U.S. Air Force service members stationed at RAF Woodbridge reported encountering unusual lights in Rendlesham Forest. Key witnesses included Deputy Base Commander Lieutenant Colonel Charles Halt, who recorded observations on audio tape and filed an official memorandum (the "Halt Memo") to the UK Ministry of Defence.

Reported observations included:
- A luminous, metallic, triangular object in the forest
- Impression marks and elevated radiation readings at the alleged landing site
- Beams of light directed at the ground and toward the base's weapons storage area
