# Yamanaka factors (OSKM)

Oct4, Sox2, Klf4, c-Myc — the four transcription factors Yamanaka used
to induce pluripotency. In the longevity context, **transient / partial**
expression rejuvenates cells without driving them to pluripotency.

## In this wiki
- [[papers/mesenchymal-drift-cell-2025]] — OSKM reduces mesenchymal
  drift before pluripotency; even non-reprogrammable cells benefit.
